Worldwide Education

In 2019, a new primary school was built in Cempaka Village with scholarships for poor children. School enrollment rose from 60% to 95% in three years, ensuring all children received basic education.

Fairness for All People

In Merdeka City, programs encouraged girls to stay in school with awareness campaigns and scholarships. Girls' dropout rates dropped from 20% to 5% in five years, and schools improved safety and comfort.

Equal Access

Bahagia County launched free skills training for disabled and remote residents in sewing, farming, and IT. Over 500 participants found jobs or started businesses within two years.
